Our Mission

USE was founded in efforts to reduce academic disparities in college

USE is a new student organization on campus dedicated to bettering incoming student's educational experiences at college through semester-long computer science projects. We understand how difficult it can be adapting to challenging academic surroundings, and that's why we believe it's important to increase open access to the educational resources at the University of Minnesota. We aspire to ensure students don't need to look too far to find a strong support network.
