Upcoming Events

November 9th, 2020
Pen Pal project

Last week we officially launched our Pen Pal Project. The Pen Pal Project's mission is to connect students at the University of Minnesota with students across the world! By participating in this project, you will help create a database system that keeps track of student information, and maintain an algorithm that matches students with similar interest. If you are interested in this project, join our Discord server and introduce yourself in our Introduction channel!
